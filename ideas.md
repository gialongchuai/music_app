<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Neumorphism (Soft UI)
  
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Tactile Realism**: Elements should look like they are extruded from the background.
  2. **Softness**: Gentle shadows and highlights to create depth without harsh lines.
  3. **Minimalism**: Focus on the essential controls, removing clutter.
  4. **Fluidity**: Smooth transitions and interactions that mimic physical buttons.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Base**: Light gray or off-white (#E0E5EC) to allow shadows to pop.
  - **Accents**: Soft pastel colors (blue, purple) for active states, avoiding high contrast.
  - **Intent**: To create a calming, physical-like interface that feels comfortable to touch and use.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Split Screen**: Playlist on the left (inset), Player on the right (extruded).
  - **Floating Controls**: Playback controls float in the center of the player area.
  - **Rounded Everything**: High border-radius to enhance the soft feel.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Soft Shadows**: Two shadows (light and dark) to create the 3D effect.
  - **Inset Inputs**: Search bars and volume sliders appear pressed into the surface.
  - **Circular Buttons**: Play/Pause buttons as prominent, circular, extruded elements.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Press & Release**: Buttons should visually depress when clicked.
  - **Smooth Sliders**: Volume and progress bars should feel like physical sliders.

  **Animation**:
  - **Gentle Pulses**: Album art pulses slightly with the beat.
  - **Slow Fades**: Transitions between songs should be slow and smooth.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Font**: Nunito or Quicksand (Rounded sans-serif).
  - **Hierarchy**: Large, soft headings for song titles; smaller, lighter text for artists.
</idea>

  **Design Movement**: Cyberpunk / Synthwave
  
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **High Contrast**: Dark backgrounds with neon accents.
  2. **Tech-Inspired**: Glitch effects, grid lines, and digital fonts.
  3. **Immersive**: A feeling of being inside a futuristic interface.
  4. **Energy**: Dynamic visualizations and glowing elements.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Base**: Deep black or very dark purple (#050510).
  - **Accents**: Neon pink (#FF00FF), Cyan (#00FFFF), Electric Lime (#CCFF00).
  - **Intent**: To evoke energy, excitement, and a futuristic vibe.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Asymmetric Grid**: Broken grids with overlapping elements.
  - **HUD Style**: Information displayed like a Heads-Up Display.
  - **Sidebar**: Vertical navigation with glowing indicators.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Glow Effects**: Box-shadows that simulate neon light.
  - **Glitch Text**: Occasional text distortions on hover.
  - **Grid Backgrounds**: Subtle grid lines in the background.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Instant Feedback**: Snappy, digital responses to clicks.
  - **Hover Glows**: Elements light up intensely when interacted with.

  **Animation**:
  - **Glitch Transitions**: Scene changes with a digital glitch effect.
  - **Spectrum Visualizer**: Audio visualizer bars reacting to music.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Font**: Rajdhani or Orbitron (Tech/Sci-fi sans-serif).
  - **Hierarchy**: Uppercase, spaced-out headers; monospace for metadata.

<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Glassmorphism (Frosted Glass)
  
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Translucency**: Background blur to create a sense of depth and context.
  2. **Vibrancy**: Vivid background colors that shine through the glass layers.
  3. **Lightness**: Thin borders and white text to maintain an airy feel.
  4. **Layering**: Clear visual hierarchy through stacking glass panes.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Base**: Transparent white/black with backdrop-filter: blur.
  - **Background**: Abstract, colorful gradients (Purple, Blue, Pink blobs).
  - **Intent**: To create a modern, sleek, and premium look that feels lightweight.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Card-Based**: Playlist and Player are separate glass cards floating over the background.
  - **Centered Focus**: The currently playing song is the focal point.
  - **Overlay Sidebar**: Playlist slides in or sits as a semi-transparent layer.

  **Signature Elements**:
  - **Frosted Glass**: `backdrop-filter: blur(10px)` on containers.
  - **Vivid Orbs**: Moving colored orbs in the background.
  - **Thin Borders**: 1px semi-transparent white borders to define edges.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Hover Lift**: Cards slightly lift and brighten on hover.
  - **Fluid Motion**: Elements slide and fade in smoothly.

  **Animation**:
  - **Background Movement**: Slow, ambient movement of background colors.
  - **Blur Transitions**: Content changes with a blur-in/blur-out effect.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Font**: Montserrat or Poppins (Geometric sans-serif).
  - **Hierarchy**: Bold, white headings; semi-transparent white for secondary text.
</idea>
</text>
